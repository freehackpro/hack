Team Fortress 2, the work of programmers from Valve Corporation, can be characterized as a multiplayer game, with a strategic accent, team-oriented shooter in which we view the actions of the characters from the first person perspective. The authors themselves call their work „the best looking and most playable multiplayer game”. The gameplay is about performing a number of missions, which is strongly hindered by a competitive team. The new title in some elements resembles the original Team Fortress. I mean, first of all, leading a team into battle, consisting of a set of soldiers with unique abilities. Regardless of what mission we are going to carry out, it is essential to perfect cooperation between soldiers and the best use of their abilities. There are nine classes available, all known from the previous part: Heavy, Spy, Scout, Demolitions, Engineer, Medic, Sniper, Soldier and Pyro. Everyone will find something for himself. Fans of Rambo-style heroes will choose Heavy or Soldier, supporters of more subtle actions will probably choose Scout or Sniper, while those who prefer action in the second line can play the role of Medica and Engineer.

The element that will surprise players the most is certainly the graphic solution used by the game developers. Team Fortress 2 is nothing like the latest titles, usually striving to reflect reality as closely as possible. This time the authors decided to use a solution resembling a comic or cartoon movie. We are dealing with a new system of showing light effects, as well as a unique way of animating characters – full 3D but with very little detail. Thanks to this, the soldiers look like crosswords of comic book heroes and collectibles. What’s more, the heroes are shown in a decidedly exaggerated way: they have a full mustache, overly elaborate arms (symbolizing strength), morbidly emaciated figures (to make it easier to hide), etc.
